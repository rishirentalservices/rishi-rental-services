Safety Rules for Renting & Driving

# Rule Description
1 Wear a Helmet It is mandatory to wear a helmet while riding a two-wheeler in Goa.
2 Carry Valid Documents Always carry a valid driving license,Driver should have atleast 3 years driving experience.
3 Follow Speed Limits Maintain the speed limit of "highway(70 kmph)","WithIn the City (40 kmph)" in Goa traffic rules.
4 No Drunk Driving Driving under the influence of alcohol is strictly prohibited and punishable by law.
5 Parking in Designated Areas 1.Always park your rented vehicle in authorized parking zones.
2.Car should be driven within Goa.
6 Follow Seating Capacity Ensure passengers are seated as per the car's capacity.
7 Drive Only on Roads Avoid off-roading, beaches, and restricted areas.
8 Use Approved Self-Drive Cars Only government-approved self-drive cars are allowed.
9 Front Seat Belts Mandatory Both front-seat passengers must wear seat belts at all times.
10 Respect Traffic Signals Always stop at red signals and follow traffic rules.
11 Customer Responsibility for Damages The customer is fully responsible for any damages incurred to the vehicle during the rental period.
12 Security Deposit Customers should deposit ₹3000 for sedans and hatchbacks, and ₹5000 for SUVs.
